My Roots, My Story

I was born at Hospital Besar Kota Bharu, Kelantan, and spent most of my childhood growing up in Terengganu. I was raised as an adopted child in a family that has always treated me with love, trust, and support. Although my journey began differently from many others, I have always felt blessed and grateful to be surrounded by people who have shaped me with care, guidance, and strong values. I am the sixth child in the family and the only son, which has given me a unique place among my siblings. Growing up with sisters has taught me patience, understanding, and the importance of appreciating different perspectives. Recently, our family was blessed with the arrival of twins from my sister, adding new joy and excitement to our lives. Their presence reminds me how family continues to grow, evolve, and create beautiful moments that strengthen our bond.
